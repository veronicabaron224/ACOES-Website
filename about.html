Adamson Computer Engineering Society

The Adamson Computer Engineering Society is a recognized student organization (RSO) at Adamson University dedicated to becoming exemplars of excellence, service, and kindness. Over the years, ACOES continuously searches and creates ways to cultivate the talents and passions of the computer engineering body in a way that they may use their honed skills to serve others.
